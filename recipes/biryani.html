Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices (turmeric, garam masala, cumin, coriander)

Preparation Time:

1 hour

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Fry onions, add tomatoes, and cook chicken.
  3. Layer cooked rice and chicken in a pot.
  4. Steam for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve.
