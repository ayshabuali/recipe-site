Tacos Al Pastor

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tbsp achiote paste
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Corn tortillas
  • Fresh cilantro and onions

Preparation Time:

1 hour (including marination)

Instructions:

  1. Marinate pork in achiote, pineapple juice, and spices for 1 hour.
  2. Grill the pork slices until slightly charred.
  3. Chop the pork and serve in corn tortillas.
  4. Top with cilantro, onions, and pineapple.
