Crunchy Roll Sushi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 4 sheets nori (seaweed)
  • 1 cucumber (thinly sliced)
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 200g fresh salmon or tuna
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar

Preparation Time:

40 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Cook sushi rice and mix with rice vinegar.
  2. Place nori on a sushi mat and spread rice evenly.
  3. Add cucumber, avocado, and fish.
  4. Roll tightly and slice into pieces.
  5. Serve with soy sauce and wasabi.
